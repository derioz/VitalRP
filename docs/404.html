Frequently Asked Questions

Is Vital RP a whitelist server?

Yes — Vital RP is a whitelisted FiveM community. Our application process is straightforward. We want to ensure players understand our rules and are committed to serious roleplay before joining.

What is the age requirement for Vital RP?

You must be 18 years or older to join. We cover mature themes and expect maturity that aligns with an adult FiveM RP community.

How do I join Vital RP?

Visit vitalrp.net, read the rules, and submit your whitelist application. Once approved, connect via FiveM at cfx.re/join/ogpvmv.

Do I need a high-end PC to play on Vital RP?

Our assets are heavily optimized. If you can run base GTA V at 60 FPS you're good. 16GB RAM recommended for best texture loading on our FiveM server.

Is voice chat required?

Yes — a microphone is mandatory. Vital RP uses pma-voice built into the server, so no external downloads needed.
